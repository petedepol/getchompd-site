Frequently Asked Questions

How does the AI Trap Scanner work?

Take a screenshot of any subscription signup page, terms of service, or email confirmation. Chompd's AI (powered by Claude) analyses the text and flags hidden traps ‚Äî auto-renewals, cancellation barriers, price increase clauses, and anything else buried in the fine print. Your screenshot is processed securely and never stored.

Do I need to connect my bank account?

No. Chompd never asks for bank access, email access, or any personal login. You add subscriptions manually or by scanning screenshots. Your financial data stays on your device.

Is there a monthly subscription fee?

No. The irony of a subscription tracker that charges you monthly isn't lost on us. Chompd Pro is a one-time purchase of ‚Ç¨4.99. The free tier is genuinely free forever with unlimited subscription tracking.

What languages does Chompd support?

English, Polish, German, French, and Spanish ‚Äî with more coming based on user feedback.

How is my data protected?

Chompd uses anonymous authentication ‚Äî no email, no name, no account. Your subscription data is stored locally on your device. Optional cloud sync uses EU-hosted servers (Supabase) and is encrypted in transit. We collect zero analytics and serve zero ads.

Can I export my data?

Yes. Chompd Pro includes CSV export so you can take your data anywhere. We don't believe in locking you in.
